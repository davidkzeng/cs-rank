Computer Science Department Rankings using PageRank

This site ranks top computer science departments by tracing where each school hires its professors from. Using data from drafty.cs.brown.edu/professors, we generate a Markov chain, where each university is represented as a state and the probability to transition from university A to university B is determined by the fraction of professors at A who got their PhD's from B.

This ranking is for experimental purposes and could use improvement. Feel free to check out github.com/davidz-eng/cs-rank for implementation details.
