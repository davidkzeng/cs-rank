Computer Science Department Rankings using PageRank

This site ranks top computer science departments by tracing where each school hires its professors from. Using data from drafty.cs.brown.edu/professors, we generate a Markov chain, where each university is represented as a state and the probability to transition to a different university is determined by the fraction of professors who got their PhD's from that university.
Note that this ranking was generated primarily for experimental purposes. Feel free to check out github.com/davidz-eng/cs-rank for implementation details.
